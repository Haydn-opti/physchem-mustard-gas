Chemical Warfare in WWI

The weapon that changed the rules of war, and launched modern cancer treatment.

Mustard gas is one of history's most devastating chemical weapons. But the same molecular properties that made it lethal also made it the unlikely origin of chemotherapy. This site explores the chemistry behind both.
