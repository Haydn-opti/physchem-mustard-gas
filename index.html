Chemical Warfare in WWI

The weapon that changed the rules of war — and accidentally launched modern cancer treatment.

Mustard gas is one of history's most devastating chemical weapons. But the same molecular properties that make it lethal also made it the unlikely origin of chemotherapy. Explore the chemistry behind both.
